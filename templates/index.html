About Us

Commuting throughout the campus becomes difficult for students specially when the campus is so big, CYKL helps students to overcome this difficulty by helping them book cycles online from one checkpoint to another within the campus.

Any student who wants to rent a cycle can login into her/his account, select the checkpoints of commute,depending upon the availibity of cycles and parking space at the checkpoints students will be alloted a specific cycle within in a specific time-frame.The student can then pick up the cycle from one checkpoint, travel to the other and submit the cycle there.

Every cycle has its own code and after every 150 rides each cycle will be inspected for maintainace. Each student is allowed to go on 15 rides per week , this is done so that most students can use the facility of CKYL to the greatest.
